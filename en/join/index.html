We recruit new members mainly on April and October.

In order to maintain the lively atmosphere of our community, we hold screening for the prospective applicants. The process of selection is as follows.

Come to at least one of our information sessions. This is for you to get first-hand knowledge of Bizjapan.

Submit an application form so that we can know more about you.

Participate in an interview.

Aside from the spring and autumn recruitment, we also recruit members throughout the year.
If you are interested, please contact from the contact form on HOME page or here.
